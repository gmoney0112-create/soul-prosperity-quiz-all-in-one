We use the information we collect to:

When you submit your name and email address through our quiz funnel or opt-in forms, you are consenting to receive email communications from Soul Prosperity Group. These emails may include your quiz results, educational content, product recommendations, and promotional offers.

You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email we send. Once you unsubscribe, we will remove you from our marketing list within 10 business days. Transactional emails related to purchases you have made may still be sent.
